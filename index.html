    Authentic Vietnamese Cuisine Experience

    🍜 Signature Dishes of Vietnam

    Pho

    🧂 Ingredients:

    • Beef bones
    • Rice noodles
    • Beef slices
    • Star anise, cinnamon
    • Onion, ginger

    👨‍🍳 Cooking Steps:

    1. Simmer beef bones for 6–8 hours.
    2. Add spices and charred onion & ginger.
    3. Cook rice noodles separately.
    4. Pour hot broth over noodles & beef.

    Banh Mi

    🧂 Ingredients:

    • Baguette
    • Grilled pork
    • Pickled carrot
    • Cucumber
    • Chili & herbs

    👨‍🍳 Cooking Steps:

    1. Grill seasoned pork.
    2. Slice baguette and spread sauce.
    3. Add meat, vegetables, and herbs.
    4. Serve fresh and crispy.

    Goi Cuon (spring roll)

    🧂 Ingredients:

    • Rice paper
    • Shrimp
    • Pork slices
    • Vermicelli
    • Fresh herbs

    👨‍🍳 Cooking Steps:

    1. Soften rice paper in water.
    2. Add shrimp, pork, noodles, herbs.
    3. Roll tightly.
    4. Serve with peanut sauce.
